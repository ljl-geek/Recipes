Beef Mushroom Erzatz Pilaf
ljl

* 2 # Ground beef (96/4)
* 1 # Canned mushrooms, sliced
* 1 quart Beef broth
* 7 cups water
* 1 cup lentils, washed
* 2 cups white rice, rinsed
* 1 cup orzo
* 1/4 cup dried onion
* Seasonings: rosemary, basil, oregano, salt.

In a 6 quart pot, brown the ground beef, then add the mushrooms, beef broth, water, dried onion and lentils. 

Bring to a boil. 

Add the rice, return to a boil, cover, cook 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 

Add the orzo, return to a boil, cover, cook for another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 

Turn off the burner, cover and let absorb for 15 minutes.

Makes approximately 152 oz.
